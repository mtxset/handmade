It's called defintitions because there could acronyms and abbreviations which are different.

acronym - uses first letter of words in phrase

NASA  - National Aeronautics and Space Administration
ZIP   - (zone improvement plan)
taser - (Thomas A. Swift's Electric Rifle)
AIDS  - (acquired immune deficiency syndrome)


abbreviation - short form of word

vol.  - volume
cm    - centimeter
min.  - minute

used in code:
sq    - squared
coeff - coefficient
dst   - destination
src   - source
inv   - inverse
4x    - simd value (128 bit - 32 byte)
